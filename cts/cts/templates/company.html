Cornerstone Technical Solutions began back in April of 2012. Cornerstone was started as a result of seeing Companies being overcharged and under delivered IT services. Employees being under-paid, under-appreciated and not having a healthy work-life balance. While making a profit is important the company started with our Founder working consulting contracts while helping customers succeed in areas of Virtualization, Storage and Data Protection technologies. In this time, we have come across like-minded individuals willing to join the team and be successful in the industry. It takes time to build a team, and after 3 years we have the core of that team in place.

At Cornerstone We do everything in our power to make sure our partners (a.k.a. customers) succeed and have a more efficient way of doing IT.
